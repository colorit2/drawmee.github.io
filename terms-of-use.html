Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22/05/2025

Hi there 👋

Thanks for using DrawMee!

We’re Befriend Studio, and we’re committed to respecting and protecting your privacy. We don’t collect more data than we need. We don’t sell your data. And we believe your data belongs to you.

This Privacy Policy explains what data we (Befriend Studio) collect and what we do with it when you use our app DrawMee.

What we collect

We aim to collect as little personal data as possible. In some cases, however, certain data may be collected to improve your experience or maintain the app's functionality.

Here’s what we may collect:

  • Usage Data – such as how often you use the app, session duration, and which features you interact with.
  • Device Information – type of device, operating system version, language settings, and crash logs.
  • Drawings & Projects – any artwork you create within DrawMee stays on your device unless you choose to export or back it up.
  • Optional Account Info – if we ever offer accounts or cloud sync in the future, we may collect your email or username. Currently, DrawMee does not require login or registration.

What we don’t collect

We do not collect:

  • Your name
  • Your DeviceId
  • Your email address (unless you contact us directly)
  • Anything you draw, unless you choose to share it with us

Why we collect

We collect data to:

  • Make DrawMee better and more stable 🛠️
  • Understand which features are useful and which ones need love 💜
  • Fix bugs and crashes quickly 🐞
  • Optionally respond to your feedback or questions

Third-party services

We may use third-party tools to help us understand usage patterns and app performance (like analytics or crash reporting services). These tools may collect anonymized data to help us improve DrawMee.

We only work with services that respect user privacy and comply with international privacy laws.

Your data, your choice

You’re in control:

  • You can delete the app anytime — all locally stored data will be removed.
  • If we ever introduce account features, you'll be able to delete your account and associated data.
  • You can always reach out to us if you have questions or want your data deleted.

Children

DrawMee is safe for all ages, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you're a parent and believe your child has shared personal info with us, please contact us and we’ll delete it.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy if DrawMee changes or if laws require it. If it’s a big update, we’ll let you know via the app or website. Small updates will just show up here.
